About Smart Syllabus Tracker

Smart Syllabus Tracker is designed to bridge the gap between academic planning and execution. Our platform helps educational institutions, faculty, and students stay on the same page regarding syllabus progress and completion.

With real-time tracking, intuitive interfaces, and role-based access, we make syllabus management efficient and transparent for everyone involved.
